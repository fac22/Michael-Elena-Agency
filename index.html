About MEA Robotics

MEA Robotics was founded in 2021 by Michael Williams and Elena Marinaki to provide modern solutions to the rising demands of everyday life. An innovative agency with the most technologically advanced team members, ready to cater every need!

Meet our team members!

  • Meet Herbie the Home Helper!

    A classic choice and one of our most popular members, Herbie is here to help you with everyday chores! From cooking and to tiding up to fixing home malfunctions, it is the assistant that came here to stay!

  • Meet Scarlett the Scheduler!

    The modern solution to balancing personal and work life! Scarlett creates the perfect schedule for you, manages the correspondence you want, and takes of your tasks. Because time is not money, it's life!

  • Meet Shane the Shopper!

    Either personal or owned by up to 3 different households, Shane takes care of your shopping list. Running out of groceries or want to buy a new pair of headphones? Consider it done!

  • Meet Vivian the Visual Assistant!

    The ultimate companion for users with impaired eyesight! Vivian is equipped with the latest advances of AI, adapts to your habits, and assists you in the most efficient and discreet way - from reading, to navigating the city!

  • Meet Chiron the Doctor!

    For all your medical needs, Chiron can be personal or owned by up to 3 different households. It helps diagnosing medical issues fast and reliably and communicates everything with your GP!

  • Meet Enzo the Entertainer!

    Planning an event and you have run out of ideas? Worry not! Enzo comes to the rescue and takes care of everything. As skilled a pianist as a potential comedian, Enzo is here to steal the show!
